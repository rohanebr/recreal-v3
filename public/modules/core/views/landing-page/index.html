Recreating Hiring

recreal aims to help businesses minimize time investment in recruitment and focus more on what they do best.

Problem

Hiring can be complicated, specially when it comes to projects with strict timelines. Despite of all the hardwork most organizations fail to hire the best candidate.

Solution

recreal allows its users to browse millions of pre-interviewed, pre-tested, linkedin authenticated resumes with ease.

Why Us

recreal runs its matching algorithm built on the best practices by HR gurus from around the world and finds the applicant that best fits your job's requirements.

Develop a Hiring Gameplan

The Features You'll Love

  • Live information like when was the resume last updated and whether the applicant is online at the moment.

  • Live chat with applicant before hiring them. After all hiring is personal.

  • Loose filters allow the employers to filter large collections of applicants on many attributes such as, location, education, skills, visa status etc.

  • Interactive dashboards for data visualization of the entire hiring process.
